About Wild Crumb

Wild Crumb started as a home baking obsession and slowly turned into a tiny microbakery. Everything is mixed, shaped, and baked by hand — no shortcuts, no weird ingredients.

Each bake starts with a bubbly sourdough starter, long fermentation, and high-quality flour. The result? Bread and bagels that are deeply flavorful, easier to digest for many people, and just…really fun to eat.
